Scents by Jas Site Concept

The Client

Scents by Jas is a retailer of a variety of perfume collections. Clients are middle income and lower income earners.

The Idea

The goal of the website was to increase conversion rate for potential visitors, to make them potential buyers.

The Concept

The goal was to be achieved by having an easy to navigate site, a site that could reflect what the potential buyers could perceive themselves as - classy.

Design Choices

It starts with the logo. A friendly and whimsical style yet, elegant and striking.

Then, a simple message to invite a sense of wonder to visitors, "Have a wonderful adventure to find your scent."

The choice of colors, all in a monochrome selection of brown was to invoke a style choice of unity.

Brown is to feel grounded, the shades of brown were to invoke a sense of calm and pride in taste. Meant to feel sophisticated but approachable.
